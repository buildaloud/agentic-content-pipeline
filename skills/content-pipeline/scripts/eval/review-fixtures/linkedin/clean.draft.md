We cut our release cycle from two weeks to two days by killing a review step nobody could explain the purpose of.

We killed it. Shipped three customer-requested fixes the same week.

The lesson isn't "delete all process." It's: if you can't say what a step catches, it isn't catching anything — it's just risk wearing a different hat.

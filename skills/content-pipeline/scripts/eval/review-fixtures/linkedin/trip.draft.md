## Why velocity matters more than people think

For a long time our team went back and forth on whether shipping fast or shipping carefully was actually the better tradeoff, and we ran through a lot of different scenarios internally before we finally landed on an answer that held up once real customer pressure and a real roadmap got involved: speed without a tight feedback loop is just risk wearing a different hat, and here's what we changed.

We cut our release cycle from two weeks to two days by killing a review step nobody could explain the purpose of, and shipped three customer-requested fixes in the same week we made the change.
